About Me

Hi, and thank you for visiting my profile. I am Zain Ramzan, a Master student in Computer Science - Industry 4.0 at the University of Pau and Adour Countries, France.

Currently, I am working as a Research Intern at Computer Science laboratory UPPA (LIUPPA) on the BEHAVE project under the supervision of Dr. Adel Noureddine, focusing on AI-driven recommendations for energy-efficient software usage. My role involves developing algorithms to categorize the software applications and make decisions based on their category and user profile.

I am passionate about applying advanced technologies to solve complex problems. I have worked on diverse projects ranging from real-time object detection to energy-efficient software systems, and I enjoy mentoring and collaborating on innovative tech solutions.
